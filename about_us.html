About Us

Papillon hair boutique lies nestled in the heart of Glasgow city centres new business district. Situated on 208 West George Street at the junction of Wellington Street. Established in 2013 by owners Jaclyn and Charl McCann, Papillon aims to offer you hairstyling and services befitting our ‘five star’ rated boutique. Our newly added beauticians ‘Be you beauty’ and ‘Emma Lou Beauty’, share our passion for excellence, friendliness and warmly welcoming you to our beauty salon.
